Creamy Rose Pasta

Prep: 25 Mins
Level: Medium
★★★★★

📌 The Secret Stash 🧂🍴

250g Pasta

1 Cup Cream

2 tbsp Butter

3 Cloves Garlic

1/2 Cup Cheese

Tomatoes

💫How to Make It✨👩‍🍳

1

Boil the Pasta

Cook your pasta in heavily salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve a small cup of pasta water before draining.

2

Sauté the Aromatics

Melt butter in a large pan and sauté the onions and garlic until they are soft and fragrant.

3

Simmer the Tomatoes

Pour in the tomato purée, dried basil, and chili flakes. Let it simmer for about 5–7 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.

4

Create the "Rose" Sauce

Lower the heat and stir in the heavy cream. Watch the sauce turn into a beautiful blush-pink color.

5

Emulsify and Toss

Add the cooked pasta and Parmesan cheese to the pan. Add a splash of the reserved pasta water to make the sauce extra glossy.

6

Garnish and Serve

Toss everything together until the pasta is perfectly coated. Top with fresh parsley and serve immediately.
