Spiced Mexican Chocolate Avocado Mousse

Prep + Resting: 75 Mins
Level: Easy
★★★★★

📌 The Secret Stash 🧂🍴

2 pcs Ripe avocados

1/2 cup Unsweetened cocoa powder

1/2 cup Maple syrup / Honey

1 tbsp Cornstarch

1/3 cupAlmond milk

1 tspVanilla extract

1/2 tspGround cinnamon

1 pinchCayenne pepper

💫How to Make It✨👩‍🍳

1

Prepare the Avocados

Scoop the flesh of two ripe avocados into a blender, ensuring there are no brown spots for a clean, green base.

2

Add the Cocoa

Sift in the cocoa powder to prevent clumps and add the maple syrup for natural sweetness.

3

Spice the Blend

Add the cinnamon, vanilla, and a tiny pinch of cayenne; the pepper won't make it "hot," just deeply flavorful.

4

Emulsify with Milk

Pour in the almond milk slowly while blending to help the mixture reach a silky, whipped consistency.

5

High-Speed Whirl

Blend on high for 2 minutes until the green color completely vanishes into a rich, dark chocolate brown.

6

Chill and Garnish

Spoon into glass bowls and refrigerate for at least one hour; serve with a sprinkle of sea salt or a cinnamon stick.
