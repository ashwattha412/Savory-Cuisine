Rose Macarons

Prep + Resting: 90 Mins
Level: Very Hard
★★★★★
View Recipe 2

📌 The Secret Stash 🧂🍴

100g Almond flour

180g Powdered sugar

2 Large egg whites

50g Granulated sugar

1/2 tspRose water

100gWhite chocolate

50mlHeavy cream.

Pink food coloring

💫How to Make It✨👩‍🍳

1

Sift the Dry Base

Sift the almond flour and powdered sugar twice through a fine mesh strainer to remove any lumps for a smooth shell.

2

Whip the Meringue

Beat egg whites until foamy, then slowly add granulated sugar until stiff, glossy peaks form that don't fall when the bowl is flipped.

3

Fold and Color

Gently fold the dry ingredients into the meringue; add a drop of pink food coloring and rose water until the batter flows like "molten lava."

4

Pipe and Rest

Pipe small circles onto a parchment-lined tray and tap the tray hard on the counter to release air bubbles; let sit for 30 minutes until a skin forms.

5

Bake the Shells

Bake at 150°C for 14–16 minutes; the shells should have visible "feet" and peel away from the paper easily once cooled.

6

Fill with Ganache

Melt white chocolate with heavy cream to create a ganache, let it thicken, then pipe it between two shells to sandwich them together.
