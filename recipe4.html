Honey Sesame Chicken Stir-Fry

Prep + Cooking: 25 Mins
Level: Medium
★★★★★

📌 The Secret Stash 🧂🍴

500g Chicken breast (cubed)

3 tbsp Honey

2 tbsp Soy Sauce

1 tbsp Cornstarch

1 tbsp Sesame Oil

Sesame Seeds

Snap Peas

💫How to Make It✨👩‍🍳

1

Dust the Chicken

Toss the chicken cubes in cornstarch and a pinch of salt until lightly coated.

2

Sear the Meat

Heat oil in a large skillet and fry the chicken until golden brown on all sides.

3

Whisk the Glaze

In a small bowl, stir together the honey, soy sauce, sesame oil, and a splash of water.

4

Sauté the Veggies

Push chicken to the side and add your greens (snap peas/broccoli), sautéing for 2 minutes.

5

Thicken the Sauce

Pour the honey mixture over everything and stir as it bubbles and turns into a thick glaze.

6

Final Garnish

Turn off the heat and sprinkle generously with toasted sesame seeds before serving.
