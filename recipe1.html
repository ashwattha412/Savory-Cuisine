Miso Peanut Ramen Bowls

Prep: 15 Mins
Level: Easy
★★★★☆
View Recipe 2

📌 The Secret Stash 🧂🍴

2 packs ramen noodles

2 tbsp miso paste

2 tbsp creamy peanut butter

1 tbsp soy sauce

1 tspsesame oil

1/2 cup warm pasta water

1 tsp minced ginger

1 clovegarlic

2 babybok choy

Chili oil

sliced green onions

toasted sesame seeds

💫How to Make It✨👩‍🍳

1

Whisk the Sauce

In a small bowl, whisk together the miso, peanut butter, ginger, garlic, soy sauce, and sesame oil.

2

Thin the Base

Slowly add the warm pasta water to the mixture and stir until it becomes a smooth, pourable cream.

3

Boil the Noodles

Cook the ramen noodles in boiling water according to the package instructions.

4

Steam the Bok Choy

Drop the bok choy halves into the same boiling water during the last 2 minutes of the noodle cook time.

5

Drain and Combine

Drain the noodles and greens, then immediately toss them in the miso-peanut sauce while still hot.

6

Garnish and Serve

Divide into bowls and top with plenty of chili oil, green onions, and sesame seeds.
