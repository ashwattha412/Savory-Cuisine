Chicken Kondattam

Prep + Cooking: 55 Mins
Level: Hard
★★★★☆
View Recipe 2

📌 The Secret Stash 🧂🍴

500g Chicken strips

1/2 cup yogurt

2 tbsp Kashmiri chili flakes

1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste

1 tspturmeric

Oil

Salt

Curry Leaves

💫How to Make It✨👩‍🍳

1

Marinate the Chicken

Mix chicken with yogurt, chili flakes, ginger-garlic paste, and salt; let sit for 30 minutes.

2

Heat the Oil

Fill a deep pan with oil and heat it until a small piece of chicken sizzles instantly.

3

Fry until Crunchy

Deep fry the marinated strips in small batches until they turn dark red and crisp.

4

Prepare the Tempering

In a separate pan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil and toss in a handful of fresh curry leaves.

5

Add Extra Spice

Throw in a few dried red chilies and a pinch of chili flakes to the tempering for extra heat.

6

Toss and Coat

Add the fried chicken to the tempering pan, toss quickly to coat, and serve hot.
